About us

Guangzhou Rongxin Electronic Product Co., Ltd. was established in Guangzhou, China in 1998. From all the start, we strive to make quality product. Through years of development, we gradually grow into one of the leading mobile phone case manufactures in China.

We focus on high-end leather and plastic cases for smart phones and tablet devices like iPhone, iPad, Samsung, Huawei, Sony and etc. In order to produce high quality products, we apply a strict management system, train our workers carefully, use advanced production facility and most of our production machines are from Japan and Germany with brands like Brother, Mitsubishi, Juki etc.

We are also proud of our strong R&D team which is made of 40 technicians and workers. They are not only experienced and creative but also energetic and efficient. With their great job, it becomes easy for us to take OEM/ODM orders and keep to provide customer fine and new designs.



Production ability is also our advantage. We have three workshops including 8 production lines for leather/PU cases and 2 for plastic covers, and we have our own tooling house and injection machines. With more than 400 workers, we can produce as many as 500,000 pieces products per month that enables us to take large order easily. And our products are sold to many different countries and regions worldwide.

Based on the principal of "STRIVE FOR THE BEST", we implement strict management system, continuously improve our craft and workmanship. In Sep, 2006, we were accredited of ISO 9001:2000 certificate. And we were awarded of ISO 9001:2008 certificate again in Sep. 2009.

Because of our quality product and service, we have won a good reputation among our customers. And we wish to set up long-term win-win business relationship with each of our customers from all over the world. Send us enquiry now and let us start a good business cooperation.
